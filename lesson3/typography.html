Typography

Heading Font: Roboto

This font provides clean readable text. Clean, readable headings bring more visitors.

Paragraph Font: Open Sans

Open Sans is a classic. Just like the Roboto font, the Open Sans font is highly readable. Sans-serif fonts work well with many different design styles.
